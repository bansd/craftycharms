Hi There!

I am so thrilled that you have decided to stop by my site. I guess you have landed on to this page to know something about me. Well then, here you go! I am mostly a card-making and scrapbooking enthusiast, however, anything crafty excites me.

I love to dabble and push my boundaries by experimenting with different mediums, tools and techniques. I have been crafting since my high school days, it all started with making cards and scrapbooking memories for family and friends, and there was no looking back. Once a craft lover, always a craft lover. I am beyond psyched to start this blog where I can share my love and passion for craft.

I am also a software engineer and a proud women in tech. I am not a website developer, but it was so much fun to design this site from writing css and html code, to designing logos in procreate. I am also hosting this site for free(thank you GitHub), so DM me on my instagram account, if you want to know more about it!
